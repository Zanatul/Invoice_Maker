Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create my first invoice?

After setting up your business profile, tap the "+" button and select "New Invoice." Fill in your client details, add items or services, and your invoice is ready to send!

Can I customize my invoice templates?

Yes! QU Invoice Maker offers several professional templates that you can customize with your business logo, colors, and branding.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. Your data is encrypted and stored locally on your device. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information. See our Privacy Policy for more details.

Can I backup my invoices?

Yes, you can enable cloud backup in the app settings to securely sync your data across devices and prevent data loss.

How do I send invoices to clients?

You can send invoices directly via email, messaging apps, or generate a PDF file to share through any platform you prefer.

What payment methods can I include?

You can add various payment methods to your invoices, including bank transfers, PayPal, checks, and any custom payment instructions.

Is there a limit to how many invoices I can create?

The free version allows you to create a limited number of invoices per month. Premium subscribers enjoy unlimited invoice creation and advanced features.

Can I track payment status?

Yes! You can mark invoices as paid, pending, or overdue, and track your business performance with built-in analytics.
